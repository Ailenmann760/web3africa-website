Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

What is Web3 Africa?
Web3 Africa is a high-impact initiative dedicated to building Africa's decentralized future through elite Web3 education, community building, and connecting African talent with global opportunities.
Who can join the SkillChain Program?
The SkillChain Program welcomes individuals across Africa passionate about Web3. We accept beginners, developers, designers, and entrepreneurs eager to build careers in the decentralized ecosystem.
How can I become a partner?
We collaborate with organizations sharing our vision. Contact us at web3africa2024@gmail.com for partnership opportunities.
What kind of events do you host?
We host technical workshops, developer bootcamps, campus meetups, and AMA sessions with industry experts to provide continuous learning and networking opportunities.
